A Message from the Heart

I never wanted things to end up this way.
I realize now how much my words and actions have affected you,
and that’s the last thing I ever wanted.

I respect your feelings, and I understand why you’re hurt.
If I could go back in time and fix everything, I would.
But all I can do now is be honest and hope you know
that I never meant to hurt your self-respect.

I can’t force anything to change, and I won’t try to.
But please know that I truly value the bond we had,
and if there ever comes a moment when we can talk again,
I will be here, always willing to listen.

No matter what happens, I will always respect the memories we shared.
I just hope, one day, you’ll know that none of this was ever intentional.
And that, in my heart, you will always be someone I cherish.
