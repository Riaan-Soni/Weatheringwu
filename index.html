For the Light in My Life

You walked in like the morning sun,
Chasing shadows, making them run.
Every word, every glance, a spark so bright,
Turning my darkness into light.

With laughter soft and kindness true,
You painted skies a golden hue.
Through storm and silence, love stood tall,
A beacon shining through it all.

So here's my heart, in words so few,
A simple song, my thanks to you.
For all the light you softly bring,
You're my warmth, my everything.
