Frommy heart to yours

No matter what has happened, one thing remains true—
our friendship still stands, and that matters more than any misunderstanding.

I know things haven’t been easy lately, but I don’t want this
moment to define everything we have shared.
We’ve laughed together, supported each other,
and built something worth holding onto.

I respect you and how you feel, and if time is what’s needed,
I am willing to give it. I just want you to know
that despite everything, you are still important to me.

True friendships are tested, but if they’re real,
they find a way forward.
No matter what happens, I am grateful for you.
